Fellowship Team

The Fellowship Team at Genet CMC Local Church is dedicated to creating a welcoming and loving environment for all members. We organize social events, gatherings, and activities that strengthen bonds within the church community.

Our Mission

To promote unity, friendship, and spiritual growth through fellowship, helping members feel connected, supported, and encouraged in their walk with Christ.

Our Activities

  • Church social events and gatherings
  • Community service projects
  • Prayer and fellowship meetings
  • Special events for youth, families, and couples
