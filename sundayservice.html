Sunday Service

Join us every Sunday for our inspiring worship service, where we come together to praise God, hear His Word, and strengthen our faith as a church family. Our Sunday Service includes worship, prayer, teaching, and fellowship for all ages.

Service Schedule

  • Morning Service: 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM
  • Children’s Sunday School: 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Evening Service: 5:00 PM – 6:30 PM

Sunday Activities

  • Worship and Praise
  • Biblical Teaching and Sermons
  • Prayer and Testimonies
  • Children’s Programs
  • Fellowship and Community Building
