Finance Ministry

The Finance Ministry of Genet CMC Local Church ensures transparency, accountability, and faithful stewardship of all church resources. The team manages offerings, donations, and budgets to support the church’s mission and community service activities.

Our Mission

To manage the church’s finances with integrity, wisdom, and faithfulness, ensuring that every resource is used to glorify God and serve His people responsibly.

Key Responsibilities

  • Recording and reporting all financial transactions
  • Managing tithes, offerings, and donations
  • Preparing and monitoring the annual budget
  • Ensuring transparency and accountability in all spending
  • Supporting special projects and community outreach
